Who Am I?

I'm a computer engineering student at Tanta University. I've been teaching programming for the past two years to both kids (starting from age 7) and university students — online and offline.

Currently, I work as a programming instructor with students from more than five countries. My teaching style focuses on real understanding rather than memorization, and I incorporate quizzes, challenges, and games to keep learning interactive and fun.

I'm passionate about learning and continuous improvement. While my main focus is front-end development, I'm currently working on developing my back-end skills as well. My goal is to make programming simple, engaging, and accessible for everyone.
